About Us

At Your Restaurant, we bring authentic flavors from the heart of Himachal Pradesh to your plate. Our mission is to serve fresh, delicious meals that leave a lasting impression on our guests. From Hamirpur to Khulri, each branch is dedicated to quality, hospitality, and tradition.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Your Restaurant began with a simple idea: to share the rich culinary heritage of Himachal with everyone. Our chefs use fresh ingredients, traditional recipes, and modern techniques to craft unforgettable meals.

Our Vision

We aim to create a welcoming space for families, friends, and food lovers, where every visit feels like home. Our goal is to expand our love for food across the region while maintaining the highest standards of taste and service.
